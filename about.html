About The Shelf Report

What this is

The Shelf Report is a collector-run notebook covering Nintendo amiibo — what they unlock, how to care for them, how to spot fakes, and (over time) new releases and restocks worth knowing about. It's written by a fan, for fans, and isn't affiliated with Nintendo.
